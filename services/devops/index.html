DevOps

A lightweight yet powerful stack for early startups.

Having a good DevOps stack is one of the most important aspects of early startups. Let us handle your code-to-production pipeline, and you can focus on what's important, your product. Our custom DevOps stack includes:

Gogs

Every pipeline starts with your code. Gogs is a self-hosted alternative to enterprise GitHub and Bitbucket solutions.

Drone

The heart of our DevOps stack, it takes care of testing and converting your code into artifacts that can be used into following stages of the pipeline.

Nexus OSS / Artifactory

Stores and serves your artifacts across the pipeline. Nexus OSS is a good open-source solution that works for most people. Artifactory, while not free, provides a more scalable solution as your business grows.

Wiki.js

The knowledge base is were the team comes to learn and share. Wiki.js is perfect for small teams that like to stay organized.

Contact us for more information about getting your own stack, training, or support.
